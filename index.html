Who We Are

More Than a Church —
We're a Family

Jesus House Silicon Valley is a Spirit-filled, multicultural community of believers rooted in the love of Christ. We exist to connect people with God and with each other through authentic worship, biblical teaching, and transforming community.

Spirit-Led Worship

Dynamic, passionate worship that draws you into the presence of God.

Biblical Teaching

Relevant, grounded messages that apply the Word of God to everyday life.

Real Community

Life-giving connections through small groups, serving, and shared mission.

What to Expect

When you come expect to feel the love of God and His power. Anticipate a supernatural experience of wisdom, grace, and family. You will feel right at home as you engage with our community, empowering individuals, families, communities, and cities.

Our Vision

To know God and to make Him known through His Word and His power.

Our Mission

Making Heavens Rejoice and Transforming the earth.

Conference

He-Motion / She-Motion Conference

He-Motion / She-Motion is our flagship annual conference — open to everyone regardless of relationship status. Whether you are married, single, a single parent, widowed, or divorced, this conference is for you. Expect powerful worship, prophetic ministry, life-changing teaching, and deep fellowship as we come together as one family to encounter God and be strengthened in our walk with Him. This is one of the most anticipated events on our calendar — do not miss it.

Prayer & Prophecy

Annual Prophetic Solution — Patmos

"Maintain Your Steadfastness" — Patmos is our annual prophetic prayer gathering inspired by the Apostle John's experience on the Isle of Patmos. It is a dedicated season of focused prayer, prophetic declarations, and strategic intercession. Believers come together to seek God's word and direction for their lives, families, and the church for the season ahead. Come ready to encounter God and receive a fresh word.
