How It Began

A Seed Planted
in Silicon Valley

Jesus House Silicon Valley began as a small, faith-filled gathering of believers who believed that God had a purpose for His church in the heart of the Bay Area. What started as a handful of people meeting together in a living room grew into something far greater than anyone could have imagined.

Led by a conviction that the people of Silicon Valley — one of the most diverse and influential regions on earth — needed a place to encounter the living God, the founders of this church stepped out in faith and planted a seed that has been growing ever since.

Today, Jesus House Silicon Valley stands as a testament to God's faithfulness — a Spirit-filled community rooted in the Word, committed to worship, and dedicated to transforming lives one by one.

A Journey of Faithfulness

The Beginning

The Church is Founded

A small group of believers, united by faith and a vision for Silicon Valley, gathered together for the first time under the banner of the Redeemed Christian Church of God. The foundation was laid — not just of a local assembly, but of a family.

Early Growth

Taking Root in San Jose

As the congregation grew, so did its impact. Families joined, lives were changed, and the church began to put down deep roots in the San Jose community. Sunday services grew in attendance and fervour, and the first outreach programmes were launched.

Expansion

A Home on Piercy Road

The church found its home at 474 Piercy Road, San Jose — a base from which to worship, disciple, and reach out to the surrounding Bay Area. The new location opened the door for expanded programmes, conferences, and ministry outreach.

Flourishing

Annual Conferences & Wider Impact

The He-Motion and Triumphant Women conferences became landmark events on the Bay Area Christian calendar. Through the Shepherds Care Foundation, the church extended practical compassion into the broader community, impacting lives well beyond its walls.

Today

12 Years and Still Growing

Over twelve years on, Jesus House Silicon Valley is a thriving, multicultural family of believers — worshipping together, growing in the Word, and making a real difference in Silicon Valley and beyond. The best is still ahead.
