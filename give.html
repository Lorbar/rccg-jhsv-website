Common Questions

Is my gift tax-deductible?
Yes. RCCG Jesus House Silicon Valley is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. You will receive a tax receipt by email.
Is it secure?
All online transactions are encrypted with SSL and processed through a PCI-compliant payment gateway.
Can I manage recurring gifts?
Yes. After your first gift you'll receive login details to manage, pause, or cancel your recurring giving at any time.
Questions?
Contact us at give@rccgjhsv.org or speak to an usher on Sunday.
