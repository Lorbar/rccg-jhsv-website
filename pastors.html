Meet Our Pastors

Shepherds called and appointed to lead, serve, and equip the people of Jesus House Silicon Valley.

Senior Pastor

Pastor Bayo
Asogba

Pastor Bayo Asogba is the Senior Pastor of RCCG Jesus House Silicon Valley, San Jose, California. He is a Spirit-filled servant of God with a heart for the people of the Bay Area and beyond. Under his leadership, the church has grown into a vibrant, multicultural community committed to worship, the Word, and transforming lives for the glory of God.

He is passionate about equipping every believer to discover their God-given purpose, and his teaching ministry is marked by clarity, depth, and practical application of Scripture.

"We believe every person who walks through our doors carries a unique destiny. Our mission is to help you discover it, develop it, and deploy it for God's glory and the good of this city." — Pastor Bayo Asogba

Pastor (Women's Ministry)

Pastor Mrs Yemisi
Asogba

Pastor Mrs Yemisi Asogba serves alongside her husband as a dedicated minister and shepherd at RCCG Jesus House Silicon Valley. She leads the women's ministry with passion and grace, championing the spiritual growth, healing, and empowerment of women in the church and community.

Known for her warmth, wisdom, and prophetic insight, Pastor Yemisi is a pillar of strength and encouragement to the congregation. She also leads the Triumphant Women Annual Conference — a gathering that has impacted the lives of many women across the Bay Area.

"Every woman is a vessel of honour. God has placed gifts, grace, and greatness within you — it's time to arise and walk in it." — Pastor Mrs Yemisi Asogba
